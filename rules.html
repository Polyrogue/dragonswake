RULES

This West Marches Style campaign is played using the Dungeons 's Dragons 5th Edition ruleset.

Players

  1. Characters can die. If you play foolishly, a character dies and they haven’t accumulated enough personal funds for a revival, you are out of luck.
  2. Experience is awarded based on the DMG plus non-combat encounters that the DM will award on a case-by-case basis.
  3. Quests/Rumours will have fixed level difficulties. They will not be rebalanced for parties.
  4. Players can not play with the same group three times in a row.
  5. Player activity is required. If you do not actively roleplay in the channel or do not attempt to arrange/join a game at least every 2 months then your Dragon’s Wake Player role will be removed and you have to reapply. No worries! Your character data will not be removed, your faithful PC will be waiting for you when you get back!
  6. Do not alter your character sheet to include currency, xp, stats, abilities aside from what the dragon's wake DMs have explicitly allowed. If you cheat in any way, you are subject to be removed from Dragon's Wake permanently. Do not push the limits on this, we want to have a fun and fair game for everyone.

Dungeon Masters

  1. Dungeon Masters are allowed to play as PCs, but not in their own quests and are not allowed to contribute to the knowledge pool or maps. Metagaming is banned.
  2. It is not the Dungeon Master's job to set up games. That falls entirely on the players. It’s the DM’s job to be a part of a game that players will arrange.
  3. It is important to stick to the lore of the world and rules of the game when creating Quests and NPCs. If you’re unsure or want to try something experimental please do not hesitate to consult the rest of the team.
